The call for a more distributed web has never been stronger. Whether it's individual concerns around privacy, censorship and centralized power or corporate concerns around stability, scalability and security people are moving away from the current monolithic services of today and toward a pattern of "connected autonomy" that's more reminiscent of the original vision of the "world wide web".

We buid the tools, services, frameworks and applications that support that movement. We aim to bring distributed, decentralized technology to the massed by making it accessible, affordable and easily deployable. Whether you need freedom or fault-tolerance, in the cloud or on the edge - we've got your back.
